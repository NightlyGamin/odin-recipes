Lasagna Recipe

This is lasagna, straight from the oven.

This lasagna was home cooked.

The ingredients were all farm fresh.

Ingredients

  • Basil
  • Tomatoes
  • Cottage cheese
  • Lasagna noodles

      • Making the lasagna

      1. Make the sauce
      2. Cook the noodles

      5. Serve fresh out of the oven
